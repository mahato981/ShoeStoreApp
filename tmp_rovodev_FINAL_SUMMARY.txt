Why GravityCompat instead of Gravity?
--------------------------------------
For DrawerLayout (part of AndroidX), you must use GravityCompat.START and
GravityCompat.END instead of Gravity.START and Gravity.END. This ensures
proper RTL (Right-to-Left) language support.

GravityCompat.START → Left in LTR, Right in RTL
GravityCompat.END → Right in LTR, Left in RTL

This is a lint requirement for AndroidX compatibility.
